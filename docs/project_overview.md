# Project Overview

## Objective

To analyze real-world travel booking data and generate actionable insights to improve marketing efficiency, product strategy, and overall business performance.

## Key Questions

1. Which acquisition channels drive the highest revenue and booking efficiency?
2. Which destinations generate the most demand and revenue contribution?
3. Which product types are associated with higher booking value?

## Insights

* **Google Ads and Direct channels contribute the highest share of total revenue**, indicating strong conversion performance and higher-value bookings.
* **OTA channels drive higher booking volume but lower average booking value**, suggesting lower efficiency and potential margin trade-offs.
* **Popular destinations such as New York and Las Vegas generate the highest booking demand**, while certain international destinations show higher average booking value.
* **Package and Hotel products tend to have higher average booking value compared to Flights**, indicating stronger revenue potential per transaction.

## Business Impact

The analysis can support data-driven decision-making by:

* Optimizing marketing budget allocation toward high-performing acquisition channels
* Improving customer targeting strategies based on channel and booking behavior
* Enhancing product and pricing strategies by focusing on high-value offerings
* Supporting strategic planning through better understanding of demand patterns and revenue drivers
